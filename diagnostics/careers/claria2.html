CLARIA NIPT can now screen for
Twins, Egg donor and surrogate pregnancies

For the first time in India
Claria NIPT determines

Zygosity information
Individual foetal fractions for dizygotic twins
Monosomy X risk for monozygotic twins

Sample requirement: 20 ml of blood in Streck Tubes
Turn around time (TAT): 16 days
Important:
Claria NIPT is applicable for twin pregnancies and not for triples or higher order pregnancies
Only singleton pregnancies in egg and surrogate cases
Laboratory should be informed before sample collection for checking feasibility and sample shipment logistics.
Claria NIPT does not report gender of the foetus
Mandatory:
TRF: All fields of the Test Requisition From must be duly filled in
Sample tubes: Name of the patient and date of birth must be mentioned on Streck tubes
